Text Encryption




This website allows you to encrypt your text using a shift cipher, also known as a Caesar's code or cipher. Enter the text you want to encrypt, choose the shift amount, and select the direction of encryption (forwards or backwards). Once you submit the form, the encrypted text will be displayed below. You can use this tool to have fun with encoding and decoding secret messages.
DON'T USE THIS TO ENCRYPT PERSONAL DATA OR INFORMATION, AS IT IS VERY EASY TO BREAK A CAESAR CIPHER, EVEN IF YOU CHANGE THE SHIFT TO A DIFFERENT NUMBER. USING A MORE MODERN FORM OF ENCRYPTION WOULD BE BETTER.
